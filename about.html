About our Project

Project Overview

Welcome to our UFO Sightings Analysis Project. Our mission is to explore, analyze, and visualize reports of unidentified flying objects (UFOs) from around the world. Using advanced statistical statistical techniques and R programming, we delve into decades of sighting reports to uncover patterns and insights.

Our Goals

  1. Data Collection: Compile a comprehensive dataset of UFO sightings from various sources.

  2. Data Cleaning and Preparation: Utilize R to clean and prepare the data for analysis.

  3. Analysis: Analyze the data to identify trends, hotspots, and temporal patterns in UFO sightings.

  4. Visualization: Create interactive visualizations to present our findings in an accessible and engaging manner.

Methodology

Our approach combines descriptive statistics, geospatial analysis, and time series analysis. We use R pacages like dplyr, tidyverse for data manipulation, ggplot2, plotly for visualization, and leaflet for interactive mapping.
